## What is XO?

XO is an AI-native development environment that eliminates the complexity of traditional software creation. Everything happens in your browser — no setup, no downloads, no limits.

Whether you're a non-technical founder, a solo builder, or a seasoned developer, XO adapts to your skill level and scales with your ambition.
