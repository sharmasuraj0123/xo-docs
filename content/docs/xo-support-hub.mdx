The XO Support Hub is a Custom GPT-powered assistant designed to streamline bug reporting and feedback submission.

## What It Does

- Collects bug reports with structured titles and descriptions
- Gathers user feedback and improvement suggestions
- Provides guided writing assistance when you need help articulating your issue

## How It Works

Visit the support hub and describe your problem or idea conversationally. The GPT assistant generates a suggested title and description — you review, edit, or expand it before confirming submission. An optional email field lets you request follow-up.

**Example:** When you report "The deployment logs page keeps timing out", the system suggests the title "Deployment logs timeout issue" with a description, then prompts for confirmation.

<Callout type="info">
  Every submission goes directly into XO's support system. Faster and easier than filling forms manually.
</Callout>
