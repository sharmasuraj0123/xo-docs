N8N Workflows is a one-click deployment tool. Launch and run your own n8n instance instantly — no manual setup, configuration, or hosting required.

<VideoEmbed id="090d0bb332bb4365802b4089c998fad2" title="N8N Workflows — Video Walkthrough" />

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Access XO Launchpad

    Go to [launchpad.xo.builders](https://launchpad.xo.builders) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Open Templates

    Navigate to **Templates** via the sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Launch N8N

    Locate **N8N Workflows** and click **Launch Now**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Configure Your Deployment

    Enter a project name. Set volume persistence to **Persistent** if you want workflows and settings to survive restarts.

    Click **Create Deployment**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Wait for Deployment

    Allow 5–10 minutes for the deployment to complete.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Open Your Instance

    Click the dashboard link once ready, complete initial account registration, and start building automations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  Set volume persistence to **Persistent** to ensure your workflows and settings survive container restarts.
</Callout>
