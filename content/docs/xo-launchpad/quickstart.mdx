Get your first deployment live in minutes.

<Callout type="warn">
  Make sure Docker Desktop is installed and running on your machine before deploying from Local. Install it from [docker.com](https://www.docker.com/products/docker-desktop/).
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Sign in to XO Launchpad

    Go to [launchpad.xo.builders](https://launchpad.xo.builders/) and sign in to your XO account.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Create a Deployment

    From your Dashboard, click **Create from Scratch**.

    Enter the deployment details: Deployment Type, Project Name, and Port.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Or Start from a Template

    Open the **Templates** tab in the sidebar and choose from pre-built templates for a faster start.

    See [Templates](/docs/xo-launchpad/templates) for available options.
  </Step>
</Steps>
