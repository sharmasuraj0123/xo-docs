## Do

**Be clear in your prompts.** Describe your product idea in simple, outcome-oriented language.

**Use visual references.** Upload screenshots or wireframes to improve design accuracy.

**Iterate frequently.** Test often and update prompts to adjust your build direction.

**Start small.** Focus on core features before expanding to full apps.

**Leverage agents.** Use XO's specialised agents (DevOps, Crypto Coder) for specific tasks.

## Don't

**Don't expect magic from vague prompts.** "Make me a cool app" is too broad. Be specific about what you want.

**Don't skip testing.** Always validate agent-generated outputs before shipping.

**Don't hardcode too early.** Let XO generate dynamic components before manual intervention.

**Don't overload in one go.** Break down features over multiple prompts for best results.

<Callout type="info">
  The more specific your prompt, the better your result. Think like you're briefing your smartest engineer.
</Callout>
