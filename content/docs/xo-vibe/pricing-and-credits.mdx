XO uses a flexible **credit-based pricing system** designed to scale with your creativity. Credits are consumed only when tasks are actively processed — so you get the most out of every interaction.

## What Are Credits?

Credits are the standard unit of usage on XO. The more complex or resource-intensive the task, the more credits it requires.

## How Do Credits Work?

Credits are primarily consumed by:

- **LLM Tokens** — For prompt parsing, code generation, and agent collaboration
- **Virtual Machines & Kubernetes** — For file operations, cloud deployments, and CI/CD pipelines
- **Third-Party APIs** — For external data access, blockchain queries, analytics, and more

<Callout type="info">
  Credits are only consumed during active task execution. Completed tasks, saved projects, and deployed apps do not consume credits.
</Callout>

## Plans

**Free Plan**
- 500 free credits (lifetime)
- 1 active app
- Community support

**Starter Plan — $10/month**
- 1,100 monthly credits
- 3 active apps
- Basic support

**Pro Plan — $50/month**
- 5,500 monthly credits
- 10 active apps
- Priority support
- Access to experimental agents

## Add-On Credits (paid members only)

- 2,000 credits — $20
- 10,000 credits — $100

Add-on credits **never expire** and are used after your monthly credits run out.

## Do Credits Expire?

| Credit Type | Expiry | Who gets them? |
|---|---|---|
| Free Credits | Never | Given at sign-up |
| Monthly Credits | Reset monthly | Starter/Pro plans |
| Add-On Credits | Never (while subscribed) | Purchased separately |

Credits are consumed in this order: **Monthly → Add-On → Free**
